Robert Keller

Engineer, Designer, and Musician

Hello! My name is Robert Keller. I like writing code, exploring music, and making/playing games. I am a Software Engineer at Ascendant Studios. I helped push Immortals of Aveum - a AAA magic-based first person shooter made in UE5 - through its final year of development, working on a wide range of systems including combat, quests, crash analytics, and level systems. Now I'm working on other cool unannounced projects on a continuous quest to broaden my knowledge base and strengthen my game development skills.
